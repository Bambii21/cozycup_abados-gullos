About Us

Once upon a time, we drank so much coffee we decided to turn it into a career. And here we are. We started with a dream, a few bags of beans, and a coffee machine that sounded like it might explode. Over time, we figured out how to make great coffee without breaking any equipment (most of the time). Now, we’re the spot where caffeine lovers unite—whether you’re here for a quick fix, a long chat, or just to avoid doing chores at home. We’ve come a long way, but one thing hasn’t changed: we’re all about good coffee, good vibes, and bad jokes. Come for the caffeine, stay for the company
